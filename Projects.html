Tip Case Force Measurement

Customers were reporting difficulty opening cases. After taking samples from stock, I measured them. Using a force gauge, the pounds of force required to push the button latch were recorded in Excel at a high force. After, I formed graphical reports in Minitab and formed a summary of the various issues discovered and the next steps, presenting to the rest of the team. My testing and reccomendations for the cases design resulted in the case being adjusted, and then also gave reason for engineers to redesign the case entirely.

Statistical Process Control

The probes at Waygate collected Statistical data from the probe, such as head centering data. However, this data did not have an easy way to be accessed, other than physically going into the database to grab it. So, I created applications to give quality insight into our processes previously inaccessible. Built in C# and WPF and using LiveCharts, the program to create XR Charts using head centering data from SQLITE Program to output data from handset to data tables.
